Mission/Vision

Mission Statement:

Our mission is to work for a better society by promoting open hardware, software, networks, data and knowledge. Vision Statement:

Our Vision is a future where education, digital tools and knowledge empower people to share ideas and technical skills. Values

In all its actions and efforts, Tilda Center is guided by these values:

Collaboration: Working effectively across disciplines, organizations and communities to combine efforts and share information in pursuit of shared goals;
